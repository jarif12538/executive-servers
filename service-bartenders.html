Bartenders

Overview

Our Bartenders are skilled professionals who craft quality beverages while delivering exceptional guest service. They manage high-volume service, handle cash operations, and create memorable experiences at bars, events, and private functions. A great bartender is part mixologist, part hospitality expert, and all professional.

What We Offer

Premium Compensation

High hourly rates plus tips and bonuses at high-volume venues and prestigious events.

Variety of Venues

Work at fine dining restaurants, event venues, upscale bars, casinos, and private functions.

Professional Growth

Opportunities for advancement into bar management and training positions.

Industry Networking

Build connections with venue managers, event planners, and industry professionals.

Key Responsibilities

  • Mix and serve cocktails, beer, and wine to professional standards
  • Manage bar operations and cleanliness
  • Take orders and manage guest interactions
  • Handle cash and payment processing
  • Maintain inventory and monitor stock levels
  • Follow proper pouring and portion control standards

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • 2+ years bartending experience
  • Mixology knowledge and drink recipes
  • Liquor license certification (or willingness to obtain)
  • Strong customer service and communication skills
  • Cash handling and basic math abilities
  • Ability to multitask in high-volume settings

Why Choose Executive Server for Bartending Work?

  • Premium Hourly Rates: Competitive wages that reflect your expertise
  • Tip Opportunities: Work high-volume venues where tips are substantial
  • Flexible Scheduling: Choose events and shifts that work for you
  • Prestige Venues: Bartend at upscale restaurants, casinos, and luxury events
  • Professional Development: Access to training and certification programs
  • Networking: Build relationships with industry decision-makers

Ideal for These Industries

  • Fine Dining Restaurants
  • Casinos & Gaming Facilities
  • Events & Banquets
  • Cruise Lines
