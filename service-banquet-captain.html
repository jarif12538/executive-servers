Banquet Captain

Overview

Our Banquet Captains are seasoned professionals who lead service teams with precision and expertise. They manage event flow, coordinate staff, ensure guest satisfaction, and maintain the highest standards of hospitality. A true banquet captain is the backbone of exceptional event service, ensuring every detail is executed flawlessly.

What We Offer

Team Leadership

Experienced captains who guide service teams with confidence and command respect from staff and guests.

Event Expertise

Deep knowledge of different event types, service styles, and hospitality protocols.

Premium Compensation

Competitive hourly rates reflecting leadership experience and specialized skills.

Prestige Events

Opportunities to work high-profile events and establish yourself as an industry leader.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead and supervise banquet service teams
  • Coordinate with kitchen and event planning staff
  • Manage guest experience and handle special requests
  • Train and mentor service staff during events
  • Ensure adherence to service standards and timing
  • Monitor event setup, service flow, and breakdown

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • 5+ years hospitality service experience
  • Strong leadership and communication skills
  • Knowledge of service protocols and etiquette
  • Ability to manage high-pressure situations
  • Excellent problem-solving abilities
  • Professional appearance and demeanor

Why Choose Executive Server for Banquet Captain Roles?

  • Premium Compensation: Top-tier hourly rates reflecting your leadership expertise
  • Prestigious Events: Work high-profile banquets, galas, and corporate functions
  • Flexible Scheduling: Choose event-based work that fits your lifestyle
  • Career Growth: Opportunity to advance into management positions
  • Professional Development: Training programs and industry certifications
  • Network Building: Connect with venue managers and event planners

Ideal for These Industries
