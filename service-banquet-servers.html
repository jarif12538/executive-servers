Banquet Servers

Overview

Our Banquet Servers are trained professionals who deliver exceptional service during all types of events. From cocktail receptions to formal dinners, they ensure seamless service with attention to detail and guest satisfaction. Each server is committed to excellence and trained to exceed expectations.

What We Offer

Professional Training

Comprehensive training in service techniques, etiquette, and guest interaction standards.

Event Variety

Opportunities to work diverse events from formal galas to casual receptions.

Flexible Scheduling

Choose your own hours and work around your personal commitments.

Competitive Pay

Fair wages plus opportunity to earn tips and bonuses at special events.

Key Responsibilities

  • Deliver food and beverages to guests with professionalism
  • Set tables according to service standards
  • Clear and reset tables between courses
  • Address guest requests and special needs
  • Maintain proper etiquette and presentation
  • Support event coordinators and banquet captains

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • 2+ years food service experience
  • Knowledge of proper table service techniques
  • Ability to work efficiently in fast-paced environments
  • Strong customer service skills
  • Physical stamina and attention to detail
  • Professional appearance

Why Choose Executive Server for Banquet Server Work?

  • Competitive Compensation: Fair hourly wages with tip opportunities
  • Regular Events: Consistent work throughout the year
  • Flexible Scheduling: Choose events that fit your schedule
  • Professional Environment: Work at premium venues with high-quality events
  • Networking: Build connections in the hospitality industry
  • Training Support: Continuous education in service excellence

Ideal for These Industries

  • Hotels & Resorts
  • Fine Dining Restaurants
  • Events & Banquets
  • Casinos & Gaming
