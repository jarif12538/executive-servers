Stewards

Overview

Our Stewards are essential to any hospitality kitchen operation. They ensure kitchen cleanliness, handle dishwashing, assist with food preparation, and support the back-of-house operations that keep service running smoothly. A dedicated steward is the foundation of kitchen efficiency and food safety.

What We Offer

Kitchen Support

Critical behind-the-scenes work that keeps kitchens running efficiently and safely.

Food Safety Training

Training in sanitation standards and food safety protocols required for professional kitchens.

Regular Work

Steady, consistent employment with stable schedules in busy kitchen environments.

Career Advancement

Opportunity to grow into prep cook or other kitchen positions with experience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Operate commercial dishwashing equipment
  • Maintain kitchen cleanliness and sanitation standards
  • Handle pots, pans, utensils, and glassware
  • Assist with food preparation and storage
  • Remove trash and maintain proper waste disposal
  • Support kitchen staff during service

Why Choose Executive Server for Steward Staffing?

  • Reliable Kitchen Support: Stewards who help keep your back-of-house running smoothly
  • Flexible Coverage: Short-term, recurring, and seasonal staffing options
  • Professional Standards: Staff trained for cleanliness, organization, and kitchen efficiency
  • Fast Placement: Quick support for urgent or scheduled operational needs
  • Team Coordination: Stewards who work closely with culinary and service teams
  • Operational Consistency: Help maintain pace, cleanliness, and readiness in the kitchen
