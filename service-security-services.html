Security Services

Overview

Our Security Services provide professional, trained personnel to protect your guests, staff, and assets. From discreet event security to comprehensive property protection, our team ensures safety with a professional presence. We combine vigilance with hospitality, maintaining a welcoming environment while providing robust security.

What We Offer

Entry Point Control

Professional monitoring of access points with credential verification and guest screening.

Patrol Services

Visible and discreet patrols ensuring continuous property and event coverage.

Guest Safety

Professional security presence that protects guests while maintaining hospitality.

Emergency Response

Trained personnel ready to respond to incidents and coordinate with authorities.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor entry and exit points
  • Conduct security patrols and inspections
  • Respond to security incidents and emergencies
  • Check credentials and manage access control
  • Observe and report suspicious activity
  • Assist guests and provide directions as needed

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • Security certification or training preferred
  • Strong observation and communication skills
  • Ability to remain calm under pressure
  • Physical fitness and alertness
  • Knowledge of emergency procedures
  • Professional, courteous demeanor

Why Choose Executive Server for Security Services?

  • Competitive Compensation: Fair wages for professional security work
  • Flexible Scheduling: Event-based and ongoing assignments
  • Professional Training: Access to security certifications and ongoing education
  • Career Opportunities: Path to supervisory and management positions
  • Professional Uniform: Provided by Executive Server
  • Team Support: Work with professional security and hospitality teams

Ideal for These Industries
