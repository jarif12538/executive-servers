Resto Food Runner

Overview

Our Food Runners are the critical link between kitchen and dining room. They ensure dishes reach tables promptly and accurately, maintain table cleanliness, and support the entire service flow during busy service periods. Fast-moving, detail-oriented, and service-focused, our food runners keep restaurants operating at peak efficiency.

What We Offer

Fast-Paced Environment

Dynamic, energetic work in high-volume restaurants and dining establishments.

Tip Opportunities

Earn additional income through shared tips in many establishments.

Career Path

Stepping stone to server, bartender, or supervisory positions.

Regular Shifts

Consistent work schedules with opportunity for advancement and full-time roles.

Key Responsibilities

  • Deliver food orders from kitchen to tables accurately
  • Clear plates and reset tables between courses
  • Communicate between kitchen and service staff
  • Assist with table maintenance and cleanliness
  • Manage tray handling and proper plate placement
  • Respond quickly to guest and server requests

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • 1+ years food service or restaurant experience
  • Knowledge of proper food handling and service
  • Speed and accuracy in multitasking
  • Ability to work in high-energy environments
  • Good communication and teamwork skills
  • Physical ability to carry multiple plates

Why Choose Executive Server for Food Runner Positions?

  • Competitive Hourly Wages: Fair pay for essential service work
  • Tip Potential: Earn additional income through shared gratuities
  • Flexible Shifts: Various shift options to fit your schedule
  • Regular Work: Consistent employment with frequent opportunities
  • Career Growth: Clear pathway to server and leadership roles
  • Training Support: Training in food service standards and safety

Ideal for These Industries

  • Fine Dining Restaurants
  • Casual Dining Establishments
  • Hotels & Resorts
  • Events & Banquets
