Banquet Housemen

Overview

Our Banquet Housemen are the backbone of event logistics. They handle the physical setup and breakdown of event spaces, move equipment, arrange furniture, and ensure venues are ready for flawless service. From empty ballrooms to complete event setups, our housemen bring organization, strength, and attention to detail to every project.

What We Offer

Complete Event Setup

Tables, chairs, stages, decorations—we transform empty spaces into event venues.

Equipment Handling

Proper handling and storage of furniture, AV equipment, and specialized event materials.

Event Support

Throughout service, providing continuous support for setup changes and adjustments.

Reliable Breakdown

Efficient event breakdown and return of spaces to original condition.

Key Responsibilities

  • Set up tables, chairs, and event equipment
  • Arrange and configure event spaces per specifications
  • Move and store furniture and equipment
  • Handle decorations and stage setup
  • Perform breakdown and cleanup after events
  • Maintain cleanliness of event spaces throughout service

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • 1+ years event setup or hospitality experience
  • Physical strength and ability to lift heavy items
  • Attention to detail and ability to follow instructions
  • Teamwork and coordination abilities
  • Knowledge of proper equipment handling
  • Reliability and strong work ethic

Why Choose Executive Server for Banquet Houseman Work?

  • Steady Event Work: Regular event-based assignments
  • Competitive Pay: Fair hourly wages for physical work
  • Flexible Scheduling: Choose events and dates that work for you
  • Full-Time Potential: Opportunity to transition to permanent positions
  • Teamwork: Work with experienced event teams
  • Career Growth: Pathway to event coordinator or management roles

Ideal for These Industries
